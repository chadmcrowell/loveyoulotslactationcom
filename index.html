About Me

I have been supporting new parents since 2019, and then became certified as an International Board Certified Lactation Consultant (IBCLC) in 2023.
Originally from Austin, Texas, USA, I am very excited to now live in beautiful Valencia with my family.
I'm passionate about supporting families as they navigate parenthood and make informed decisions about infant feeding.
I have a Master's Degree in Public Health, and as a maternal and infant health advocate, I value health equity and evidence-based care.
While I'm currently offering my services in English, I hope to support families in Spanish soon as well.
I have experience supporting families in outpatient and community settings, perinatal units, and Level 3 neonatal intensive care units.
I also have specialized training in assessing for tethered oral ties and bottle refusal.
